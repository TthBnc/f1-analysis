# R01 AustralianGrandPrix — Weekend Summary

**Circuit:** Albert Park Grand Prix Circuit
**Conditions:** Dry qualifying; mixed/wet race with late rain and multiple safety-car interruptions
**Format:** Standard

## Headlines

- Lando Norris converted pole into victory in a high-chaos mixed-condition opener, beating Verstappen by 0.895s.
- McLaren and Mercedes emerged as early form leaders; both teams showed strong transition-race execution.
- Rookie variance was extreme: Antonelli surged P16->P4, while Hadjar crashed on formation lap and Lawson/Bortoleto crashed late.
- Williams opened with strong upside via Albon P6 in qualifying and P5 in race pace/conversion.
- Racing Bulls lost a likely points weekend through race conversion failures despite Tsunoda’s P5 grid.

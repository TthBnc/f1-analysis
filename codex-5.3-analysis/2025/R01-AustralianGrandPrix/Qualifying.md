# 2025 Australian Grand Prix — Qualifying

**Round:** 1
**Circuit:** Albert Park Grand Prix Circuit
**Date:** 2025-03-15
**Conditions:** Dry | Ambient: 23-24°C | Track: 39-42°C
**Format:** Standard weekend

---

## Session Summary

McLaren locked out the front row at Albert Park, with Lando Norris taking pole in 1:15.096 and Oscar Piastri only 0.084s behind. The headline pace signal was spread across sectors: Norris led S1 and S3, while Piastri was fastest in S2, showing the MCL39 had both rotation and traction confidence through different corner profiles.

Max Verstappen and George Russell were closest challengers but remained over four tenths from pole. Yuki Tsunoda and Alex Albon delivered standout sessions for Racing Bulls and Williams respectively, both reaching the top six and out-qualifying higher-baseline packages.

Q1 was disrupted by a red flag after Oliver Bearman went off at Turn 10 and beached in gravel. Lewis Hamilton also spun at Turn 11, bringing yellows that affected final-lap execution windows. Andrea Kimi Antonelli reported floor damage after riding the Turn 6 kerbs and was eliminated in P16.

The midfield cut line was tight: Gabriel Bortoleto progressed to Q2 in his debut, while Nico Hulkenberg, Liam Lawson and Esteban Ocon were eliminated in Q1. Isack Hadjar’s P11 and Jack Doohan’s P14 were notable rookie markers before race day volatility.
